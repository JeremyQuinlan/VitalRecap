MARKETS

RATES & FED

Powell's Harvard appearance reiterated his March 18th remarks, suggesting the Fed will look through oil shocks and tariffs as one-time events.

MARKET OUTLOOK

US equities are fading from opening highs as investors remain skeptical of Trump's optimistic Iran rhetoric—every Middle East development points to escalating conflict. Until senior officials (JD, Marco, Steve, Jared) actually board planes for Pakistan-hosted Iran talks, the "everything is swimmingly" narrative will fall on deaf ears. Tech continues trading poorly for reasons largely independent of geopolitics, while inflation pressures from the war are starting to show up globally. Most assume boots on the ground are coming within weeks.
