MARKET OUTLOOK

The rally is gaining steam on hopes that the Iran conflict could wind down without a protracted ground campaign, removing a major market overhang. While nothing is guaranteed and the situation remains fluid, lower odds of sustained military escalation are exactly what markets needed to hear. The equal-weight SPX lagging the cap-weighted index suggests this is still largely a momentum-driven move, but breadth should improve if geopolitical fears continue to fade.
