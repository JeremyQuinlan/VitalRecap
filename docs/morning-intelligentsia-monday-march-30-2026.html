MARKET OUTLOOK

US stocks are tentatively rebounding thanks to extremely oversold conditions and depressed positioning, plus some hopeful rhetoric from Trump about Iran negotiations. However, even if Trump declares victory and reaches a "deal," the attendant SPX rally should be faded—the index faced a stiff ceiling around 6900-7000 even before the war started. The conflict's fallout will have lasting ramifications for many months to come, and escalation by the US risks triggering a catastrophe with Iran ramping up economic terror throughout the Middle East.
