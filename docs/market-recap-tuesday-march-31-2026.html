MARKET OUTLOOK

The deescalation narrative around Iran is gaining serious momentum, with Trump confirming WSJ reports about exiting the conflict without militarily reopening Hormuz (significantly reducing ground troop deployment odds). This removes a major stagflationary overhang that's been plaguing markets. The $90B M&A wave this week signals corporate confidence remains intact despite macro volatility. Housing data continues showing disinflationary pressure, giving the Fed breathing room. If Friday's jobs report hits 50K+ as expected, it cements the extended pause scenario.
