MARKET OUTLOOK

The deescalation narrative around Iran is gaining serious momentum, with Trump essentially confirming WSJ reporting about ending the conflict without reopening Hormuz (which dramatically reduces ground troop deployment odds). We're seeing broad-based risk-on behavior with tech leading the charge, though the real test comes with Friday's jobs report where anything above 50K would cement Fed pause expectations.
