GEOPOLITICAL

The hard part is done. Go get your own oil!" (Trump social media). COMPANY NEWS

BIIB will acquire APLS for $41 per share cash plus up to $2 per share in contingent value rights (total ~$5.6B), representing a massive premium to Apellis's $17.09 Monday close. The deal is expected to be increasingly accretive starting in 2027 and meaningfully boost Biogen's EPS growth through the decade.

LLY is buying CNTA for $38 per share cash plus up to $9 per share CVRs (total potential value $47 vs. Monday's $27.58 close, worth up to $7.8B total). Centessa develops treatments for excessive daytime sleepiness and other neurological conditions.

MKC delivered upside with Q1 EPS of 66 cents vs. 59 cents expected, driven by revenue of $1.87B vs. $1.79B consensus. Organic growth of 1.2% fell slightly short of 1.4% expectations due to Flavor Solutions pressure, but gross margins expanded 100 basis points (20 basis points ahead of estimates). The lack of guidance raise despite the beat might disappoint, though multiple reports suggest a Unilever deal announcement is imminent.

CEG is targeting 20%+ base EPS growth from 2026-2029 and boosted its buyback authorization to $5B. For 2026, the company sees EPS of $11-12 (midpoint $11.50), slightly below the $11.72 consensus.

FDS beat Q2 EPS expectations (4.46 vs. 4.37) on strong 6.8% organic revenue growth, though operating margins fell 230 basis points to 35% vs. 35.5% expected. The company raised full-year EPS guidance to $17.25-17.75 from the prior $16.90-17.60.

SNX crushed Q1 expectations with EPS of 4.73 vs. 3.29 consensus on robust sales of $17.16B vs. $15.55B expected. Q2 guidance midpoints are well ahead of Street estimates.

NKE insists it won't sell Converse despite Authentic Brands expressing interest (Bloomberg). Given Converse's struggles (consensus models -25% revenue in F26 following -19% and -14% declines), investors would welcome a divestiture though it wouldn't bring significant proceeds.

Whoop raised $575M at a $10.1B valuation, nearly tripling its 2021 valuation of $3.6B as the fitness wearable company heads toward an IPO (Bloomberg).
