MARKETS

The SPX climbed +2.51% while the equal-weight version gained +2.35%. The Nasdaq advanced +2.80%, the Dow jumped +2.85%, and the R2K spiked +2.97%. RATES & FED

Treasuries barely budged despite the equity rally and oil collapse, with yields flat to down just 2 basis points across the curve — a concerning warning sign about inflation persistence. The market is pricing in only ~7 basis points worth of Fed cuts this year.

MARKET OUTLOOK

The ceasefire skepticism and technical imbalances point to more equity upside near-term, but the 6900-7000 range will be fierce resistance (it was struggling there even before the war). The inability of Treasuries to rally despite the ceasefire is a massive red flag, suggesting the war's inflationary effects could be larger and more durable than anticipated. Friday's CPI reading expected to show sharp acceleration will be the next major test.
