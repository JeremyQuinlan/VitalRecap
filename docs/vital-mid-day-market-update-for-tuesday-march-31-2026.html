RATES & FED

Treasury yields are flat to down 4 basis points while the market is pricing in 7 basis points of Fed rate cuts this year. The bond market appears to be taking the Iran developments in stride, with yields showing little reaction to the geopolitical news flow.

MARKET OUTLOOK

The rally is being driven primarily by hopes that Trump could wind down the Iran war in the relatively near-term without securing Hormuz (per WSJ reporting that Trump effectively confirmed). This suggests lower odds of a sustained ground campaign, which had been a major market fear — not the campaign itself, but Iran's likely retaliation by escalating attacks on critical regional economic infrastructure. Of course, nothing is guaranteed and the Iran situation remains extremely fluid. The equal-weight SPX is lagging at just 57 basis points, suggesting this is more of a large-cap driven move.
