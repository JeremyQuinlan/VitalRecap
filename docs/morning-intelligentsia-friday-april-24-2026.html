Our view on the SPX right here is neutral at best. Q1 earnings season has been undoubtedly strong and should help preserve a large chunk of recent advances, though only ~120 S&P 500 companies have reported—there's still a lot to go. Even if everything ended today, there's going to be months-long stagflationary fallout from what's already transpired. The best-case Iran outcome seems like a vague, non-binding JCPOA-like understanding that fails to address core underlying sources of contention.
